# <a name="add-resources-project-service"></a><span data-ttu-id="51843-103">Tilføje ressourcer (Project Service)</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="51843-103">Add resources (Project Service)</span></span>

[!INCLUDE[cc-applies-to-psa-app-1x-2x](../includes/cc-applies-to-psa-app-1x-2x.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="51843-104">Tilføj ressourcer, så du kan reservere dem til projekter.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="51843-104">Add resources so you can book them for projects.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="51843-105">Ressourcer kan omfatte brugere, kontaktpersoner, firmaer, grupper eller udstyr – alt hvad du vil medtage i projektplanlægning og -fakturering.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="51843-105">Resources can include users, contacts, accounts, groups, or equipment—anything that you might want to include in your project scheduling and invoicing.</span></span>  
  
<span data-ttu-id="51843-106">Du kan tildele ressourcer til organisationsenheder, hvis du skal have forskellige omkostningsberegning og prisfastsættelse for ressourcer til forskellige afdelinger i virksomheden.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="51843-106">You can assign resources to organizational units if you need to have different costing and pricing for resources for different divisions in your company.</span></span>  
  
1.  <span data-ttu-id="51843-107">Gå til **Project Service > Ressourcer**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="51843-107">Go to **Project Service > Resources.**</span></span>  
  
2.  <span data-ttu-id="51843-108">Klik på **Ny**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="51843-108">Click **New**.</span></span>  
  
3.  <span data-ttu-id="51843-109">Vælg **Ressourcetype**, og vælg derefter de ønskede indstillinger for hvert felt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="51843-109">Select the **Resource Type**, and then select the settings you want for each field.</span></span>  
  
4.  <span data-ttu-id="51843-110">Klik på **Gem** for at gemme posten, så du kan fortsætte med at redigere den.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="51843-110">Click **Save** to save the record so you can continue editing it.</span></span>  
  
5.  <span data-ttu-id="51843-111">Hvis du vil tilføje en færdighed for ressourcen, skal du klikke på **+** under **Ressourcefærdigheder**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="51843-111">To add a skill for the resource, click **+** under **Resource Skills**.</span></span>  
  
6.  <span data-ttu-id="51843-112">Udfyld felterne efter behov på det skærmbillede, der vises, og klik på knappen **Gem** i nederste højre hjørne af skærmbilledet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="51843-112">In the screen that pops up, fill in the fields as needed and click the **Save** button at the bottom right corner of the screen.</span></span>  
  
7.  <span data-ttu-id="51843-113">Fortsæt med at tilføje færdigheder efter behov.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="51843-113">Continue adding skills as necessary.</span></span>  
  
8.  <span data-ttu-id="51843-114">For at tilføje en ressourcerolle skal du klikke på **+** under **Ressourcerolle**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="51843-114">To add a resource role, click **+** under **Resource Role**.</span></span>  
  
9. <span data-ttu-id="51843-115">Klik på **Gem** i nederste højre hjørne af skærmen, når du er færdig med at foretage ændringer.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="51843-115">When you’re done making changes, click **Save** at the bottom right corner of the screen.</span></span>
